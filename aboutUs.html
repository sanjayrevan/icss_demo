About Vignan ICSS Computer Education

Vignan ICSS is a leading computer education institute in Mahabubnagar, with a reputation for providing quality education in the field of technology since 1993. Our institute has played a pivotal role in shaping the careers of students by offering both short-term and long-term computer courses.

We are the only authorized Tally training institute in Mahabubnagar District, recognized for our comprehensive courses in MS Office, C Language, Tally, DTP, and more. Our curriculum is designed to cater to the evolving needs of the IT industry, ensuring that students gain both theoretical and practical knowledge.

At Vignan ICSS, we are committed to helping students achieve their career goals by providing industry-recognized certifications and real-world skills in computer education. With experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, we prepare our students for professional success.

Our Certifications & Courses:

  • Short Term Courses: MS-Office, C Language, Java, Oracle, Visual Basic
  • Long Term Courses: DCA (Diploma in Computer Applications), PGDCA (Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications)
  • Special Programs: Desk Top Publishing (DTP), Tally Certification

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced faculty with decades of teaching experience
  • Industry-recognized certifications and diploma programs
  • Hands-on training with real-world scenarios
  • State-of-the-art computer labs and facilities
  • Career guidance and placement support
